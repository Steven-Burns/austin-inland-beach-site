Welcome to the Austin Inland Beach!

Thank you for booking our pool! We are confident you will have a pleasant time.

Please contact us through the Swimply app to ask questions or report any issues.

Your hosts will be on-site (but out-of-sight!) for the duration of your visit to see to any needs. We are always open to feedback on your experience.

Below are some instructions to make your visit a breeze.

Rules

  • No smoking anything, anywhere on the property.
  • No headfirst diving. The water is not deep enough for diving.
  • No glass on the pool deck or in the water.
  • No food or drinks in the pool or hot tub.
  • No climbing on or jumping from furniture.
  • Please play music at a respectful volume level.
  • No objects in the water except host-approved pool toys (ex: no rocks, sticks, etc.).
  • No confetti, party streamers, wedding rice, etc. Such things blow into the pool and clog the filters.
  • No dumping anything but water into patio drain.
  • No pets.

Contact the hosts via the Swimply app with any questions or issues.

Parking

Park in either driveway: the semicircular driveway on the north or the side driveway on the east.

Safety

Obey all pool rules. The rules are for your safety.

A lifesaver ring buoy is on the wall under the bar counter.

Reaching poles are on the wall by the pumps.

Please designate one or more Water Watchers to act as lifeguards. Monitor children at all times designate another adult as Water Watcher when needed.

A First Aid kit is on the bar countertop.

The hosts are CPR trained. However, they are NOT monitoring your activity. If you need to reach them in an emergency, knock and call into the house loudly.

Remove all water toys from the pool at the conclusion of your swim. Toys in the pool tempt little ones to jump back into the water unobserved.

Water toys are NOT a substitute for life preservers/vests for non-swimmers. Life preservers are not provided.

Additional Guests

All guests of any age must be registered as attendees in the Swimply app booking, no matter what activities they may participate in. This is a requirement of the liability insurance.

Waivers

Each family *must* have an adult parent/guardian sign the waiver that will be given to you upon arrival. This includes even those that do not swim.

Bathroom

Enter the house through the door that is left of the east fireplace, at the outdoor kitchen area. Follow the WC signs.

Infants / Toddlers

Please use swim diapers for children that are not yet toilet trained. Do not dispose of diapers of any sort in the toilet.

Safety

Please supervise children. No lifeguard or active monitoring is provided.

Hard surfaces can get hot in the sun and slippery when wet. Sandals are recommended.

Please do not use glass items (glasses, containers, etc.) anywhere around the pool deck or in the pool or hot tub. Broken glass is nearly invisible when wet, and very dangerous.

Grill / Outdoor Kitchen

If you added the Grill / Outdoor Kitchen Amenity to your booking, you have access to the grill, stock pot burner, east fireplace, icemaker, beverage refrigerator, sink, dining table, bar area and ceiling fans.

Fireplace

The fireplace is wood-burning. Firewood is provided. Use of hot dog / marshmallow cookers and a "hobo pie iron" is included.

Grill

The propane grill has 4 burners and a smoker. Cooking utensils are provided.

The grill can be rigged on request with heavy steel baking plates covering all or half of the cooking surface. The plates work well for baking or warming pizza, bread, tortillas, pupusas, etc.

Icemaker

The icemaker struggles to keep up with Texas heat, so in weather above 80 degrees, you are advised to bring your own ice. Feel free to use the icemaker bin as a cooler.

Toaster Oven

Fresh baked cookies or brownies by the pool? Yes please! Let the host know if you want to use the oven.

Ceiling fans

The fans are controlled with the circular pads on the east wall of the kitchen. Slide the switch to the on position. Set the speed with the 1-, 2-, or 3-dot buttons on the circular pads.

WiFi

Connect to "Burns guest" wireless network. Enter the 8-digit voucher code on the login page in the box labeled "I have a voucher."

Voucher codes are available in the Guest Goodies basket in the outdoor kitchen area. Each device requires its own code. Codes are single-use and grant access for 5 hours. Place used vouchers in the trash.

Outdoor speakers

A portable speaker with radio and phone input is provided as a free amenity. You will need to bring your own dongle appropriate for your phone to connect to the speaker.

Feel free to relocate the speaker as desired. Feel free to ask the host for assistance.

Please keep the volume at a respectful level.

Towels

If you opted for the Towel Amenity, clean towels will be provided. Please leave used towels on the bar stools at the end of your visit.

Umbrellas

Umbrellas will be set up by the hosts before you arrive. If they are not set up, then the breeze is too strong to safely leave them unfurled. Please do not open them yourself: they can be tricky in the wind.

If already opened by the hosts, the large offset umbrella can be rotated at the base. Unlatch the mast with the foot pedal at the base. Ask the host for help if needed.

Toys

Use of the floats, beach balls, and other toys provided is included. If you have toys of your own, please discuss with the host before putting them in the pool.

Video projector

If you opted for the projector and screen amenity, the host will set this up for you. Please keep the volume at a respectful level.

Canopy

If you opted for the 10x10 canopy amenity, please indicate to the host at least 24 hours before arrival where you want it placed. If you need the canopy relocated, please ask the host.

Misting Fan

If you opted for the Misting Fan amenity, please indicate to the host where you would like the fan set up (on the west patio, outdoor kitchen area, near the chaise loungers, etc.). The fan speed is adjusted with the knob on the side of the unit.

Yoga Class

If you added the private yoga class, please discuss with the host when during your booking you would like the class to be taught, and whether you prefer indoor or outdoor instruction.

Linda is certified yoga and pilates instructor.

Goodies!

Sunscreen, sunburn gel, and band-aids are available in a basket at the outdoor kitchen area.

Forget Something?

Randall's full-service grocery is about 3 miles away. Ice, drinks, food, swim diapers, etc. are available there.

Special Arrangements

If you're interested in such things as a bouncy house, wedding pavilion, your own yard games or activities, or anything else that strikes your fancy, please discuss with the host at booking time prior to your arrival. Surprise additions upon arrival may not be approved.

About the Pool

The pool is a saltwater system. This means that the water is soft and silky. The water is also gentler on skin, eyes, and swimwear as chlorine levels needed to maintain clean water are much lower than that of a regular pool. The salt concentration is about the same as human tears. 

The pool is lined with black and mother-of-pearl pebbles that are comfy to the touch and absorb sunshine to warm the water.
