Austin Inland Beach Supported Charities

We are a not-for-profit endeavor.

A minimum of 10% of gross proceeds from all bookings are donated to charity. *All* remaining proceeds are used solely to defray our operating costs. We feel we have been blessed and should do what's reasonable to share.

Cynical?

Some sour souls might scoff at the idea that we don't do this strictly for the cash. "If you're so charitable, then why isn't the pool free?" these cynics say.

Operating the pool ain't cheap. Expenses include Swimply platform fees, maintenance, chemicals, cleaning, insurance, water, repair, landscaping, endlessly replacing popped floaty toys, etc.

Lamentably, one need only look at how poorly public lands and common facilities are treated in our society to recognize that not everone is trustworthy nor acts in good faith. We feel that our booking fees are low enough to be accessible to most people of good conscience, but high enough to prevent abuses that would ruin the whole thing for everyone.

The minimum 10% that we donate comes right off the top: from our gross proceeds, whether or not there has been or will be an expense not covered by the booking revenue.

Why 10%? Because that is the law of the tithe.
